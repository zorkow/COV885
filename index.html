(Tentative) Syllabus

  • Introduction: Usability, Accessibility and the Web
  • Evaluating Accessibility of Web Documents
  • STEM Documents and their features
  • Document sources Retro-digitized, born digital and born accessible
  • Mathematical Documents
    • Conversion Tools
    • Authoring for the Web
  • Diagrams of the future
    • SVG and Semantics
    • Audio, Tactile, Audio-Tactile
    • Sonification
  • Advanced content: Interaction, Simulations, Animations
  • What's next: Existing challenges, Future Research
